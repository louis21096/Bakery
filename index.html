About us

At Bloomers, we’re passionate about creating delightful treats that warm your heart and satisfy your cravings. Our cozy little bakery is nestled in the heart of Auckland, where the aroma of freshly baked goods wafts through the air, inviting you to step inside.

🧁 Our Story: Founded by pastry chef Emma Thompson, Bloomers Bakery has been a labor of love. Emma’s journey began in her grandmother’s kitchen, where she learned the art of baking from scratch. Inspired by family recipes and a dash of creativity, she decided to share her passion with the world.
